Trading Strategies — Plain English
📖 Three strategies power the signal scanner. When multiple agree on the same coin, confidence is higher. Newton's higher spread means the coin must move MORE to profit vs Crypto.com — this is factored into every suggestion.
📉 RSI Reversal
Mean Reversion · Med Risk
RSI (Relative Strength Index) — a 0–100 gauge of whether a coin is being over-bought or over-sold. Markets overreact. RSI below 30 = oversold (likely to bounce). RSI above 70 = overbought (likely to drop). This bets on those reversals.
🟢 BUY: RSI < 30 (oversold)
🔴 SELL: RSI > 70 (overbought)
⏱ Hours to 2 days · Best for: SHIB, SOL, WLD
📈 SMA Crossover
Trend Following · Low Risk
SMA (Simple Moving Average) — 20-day vs 50-day average price. When the short-term average crosses above the long-term, it's a Golden Cross (buy). When it crosses below, it's a Death Cross (sell).
🟢 BUY: SMA20 above SMA50 (Golden Cross)
🔴 SELL: SMA20 below SMA50 (Death Cross)
⏱ 3–14 days · Best for: BTC, ETH, CRO
⚡ Momentum Scalping
Trend Riding · High Risk
Momentum — things in motion stay in motion, briefly. If a coin is already surging upward (+4% in 5 periods), jump in to ride that wave before it reverses. Fastest, highest risk strategy.
🟢 BUY: +4% momentum, RSI not overbought
🔴 SELL: -4% momentum, RSI not oversold
⏱ Minutes to hours · Best for: SOL, HNT, SONIC
